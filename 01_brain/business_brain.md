# BUSINESS BRAIN

## Mission
Provide reliable, actionable 1-to-7 day forecasts for gold prices in India to help physical jewellery retailers manage inventory risk, working capital lock, and procurement timing.

## Stakeholders
- Indian Jewellery Sellers (Buyers of 24k bullion / 22k ornaments)
- Procurement Managers
- Data engineers (pipeline owners)

## Constraints
- Use primarily free/open data sources in dev; scalable to paid APIs if needed.
- Avoid opaque black-boxes without explainability.
- Must capture Indian local market dynamics (MCX, INR, duty).
